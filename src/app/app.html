RAW began as a hobby — driven by frustration with unhealthy, overpriced fish in the local market. Instead of compromising, we started breeding our own fish to enjoy better quality. As our fish thrived, we began sharing them with fellow hobbyists — and RAW was born.

Even today, we stay true to that foundation:
✅ We sell what we breed
✅ We don’t prioritize profit over quality
✅ We aim to match local prices — with better fish

We also believe something many sellers ignore:
👉 Not every fish is right for every aquarium That’s why we interact with customers before every purchase — to ensure responsible fishkeeping.

From Natural Tanks to Your Aquarium Built by a hobbyist. Driven by quality. Focused on fish welfare.
🌿 Naturally raised
🧬 Grown from juveniles
💰 Fair, honest pricing

🌱 Our Breeding Philosophy (Unchanged Core, Now Reinforced)
We Grow Before We Breed
Our broodstock are raised from juveniles — not bought as breeding adults.

🌿 Natural Environment Breeding
We recreate natural habitats instead of using artificial setups.

🍃 In-House Nutrition
Our fish are fed in-house prepared food that mimics natural diets.

🐟 Carefully Selected Additions
While most fish we offer are bred in-house, we occasionally source a small number of species from trusted and experienced breeders whose standards align with our own. Every fish is quarantined and observed in our facility before being offered for sale, ensuring health, quality, and compatibility with our approach to fishkeeping. This allows hobbyists to find both our home-bred fish and selected complementary species in one place, without compromising on fish welfare.

✅ Core Principle
Healthy fish come from knowledge, care, and patience — not shortcuts.

At RAW, we believe buying fish should be personal, informed, and responsible — not just a quick checkout.

DOA & Refund Policy

Dead on Arrival (DOA) Policy

We take great care to ensure all livestock are healthy and securely packed before shipment.

In the rare event of a Dead on Arrival (DOA), customers must notify us within 2 hours of delivery and provide clear unboxing photos and videos showing the unopened bag and affected livestock for claim verification.

  • Claims submitted after the specified time window may not be eligible for compensation.
  • Approved DOA claims may be compensated through replacement, store credit, or refund of the affected livestock's purchase value.
  • Shipping charges are non-refundable.
  • We are not responsible for incorrect shipping information or failure to receive the package on the first delivery attempt.
  • Compensation applies only to livestock confirmed as DOA upon arrival.

By placing an order, customers acknowledge and agree to this DOA policy.
